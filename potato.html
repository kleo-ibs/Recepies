Easy Potatoes

Deliciously crispy air-fried potato wedges are perfectly seasoned and ready to serve in just over 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1½ tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ½ teaspoon parsley flakes
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cut each potato into 16 wedges.
  3. Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika, parsley, chili, salt, and pepper; mix until well combined.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes in air fryer.
